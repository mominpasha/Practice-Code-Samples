Alerts

Alerts are created with the .alert class, followed by a contextual color classes:

Success! This alert box could indicate a successful or positive action. - alert alert-success
Info! This alert box could indicate a neutral informative change or action. - alert alert-info
Warning! This alert box could indicate a warning that might need attention. - alert alert-warning
Danger! This alert box could indicate a dangerous or potentially negative action. - alert alert-danger
  Primary! Indicates an important action. - alert alert-primary
  Secondary! Indicates a slightly less important action. - alert alert-secondary
  Dark! Dark grey alert. - alert alert-dark
  Light! Light grey alert. - alert alert-light
